Questions, answered

Will this affect my credit?

We prioritize soft-pull pre-quals when available. We’ll notify you before any hard inquiry.

How fast can I get funded?

Same day possible for MCA; 1–3 days common for LOC/RBF; equipment/factoring ≈ 1 week; SBA longer but lower total cost.

What documents do I need?

ID and recent bank statements at minimum; invoices/contracts/equipment quotes or full financials as needed.

Do you work with startups?

Yes—revenue, contracts, or equipment needs help. Pre-revenue is limited, but we’ll advise candidly.
